Onboarding Process

A streamlined, transparent onboarding process designed to get you started quickly while ensuring complete understanding of our investment approach, terms, and operational procedures. We guide you through each step with clarity and professionalism.

ONBOARDING PROCEDURE

A New Standard of Transparency

1

Initial Consultation

Schedule your initial consultation with our financial advisor to discuss your investment objectives, risk tolerance, and expected liquidity needs. During this session, we'll review our strategy behavior, historical performance ranges, and operational requirements to ensure alignment with your investment goals.

This is an opportunity to ask questions, understand our approach, and determine if Opessocius is the right fit for your investment portfolio.

Account Setup & Verification

Complete the onboarding verification process by providing required documentation and personal information. Our team will guide you through creating your investor profile, setting up account configurations, and establishing access credentials.

All reporting preferences, communication channels, and account settings are configured during this stage to ensure a seamless experience moving forward.

3

Contract Review & Execution

Review the investment mandate, fee structure, risk disclosures, and operational terms in detail. Our team will walk you through each section to ensure complete understanding of the agreement.

Once all questions are addressed, finalize the agreement through a formal review process and digital or in-person signature, depending on your preference.

During this stage, you'll have the opportunity to clarify any terms, discuss fee structures, understand risk parameters, and ensure all documentation aligns with your investment objectives. Our legal and compliance teams are available to provide additional context and answer any questions you may have before proceeding.

4

Capital Funding & Activation

Transfer your initial capital to the designated account through our secure funding process. Once funds are received and verified, your account will be activated and you'll begin monitored participation in the strategy.

You'll receive transparent monthly reporting, performance updates, and have the option to schedule ongoing reviews as needed. Welcome to Opessocius.

Our secure payment infrastructure supports multiple funding methods to accommodate your preferences. All transactions are processed through encrypted channels with institutional-grade security protocols. Upon successful funding, you'll receive immediate confirmation and your account will be fully operational within 24-48 hours.
